    How to use BeerTinder!

    It's highly recommended that you "create a profile" so that all the features work properly!

    The objective of BeerTinder is to find the perfect beer for the perfect you!

    Features:

    1. It's highly recommended that you create a profile so that you have access to all the features and storage of your activities.
    2. To create a new profile, click the "profile" button. You will be prompted to enter in a username (your email address) and a unique password. The email must be a valid email and passwords must be at least 4 characters and length. After you enter in the information it is very important that you click "Send Email Verification".
    3. Once the screen is fully loaded, you will see a selection of randomized beers.
    4. For each beer selection you have the option to "Like" or "Dislike".
    5. "Liking" a beer adds that beer to your history of liked beers.
    6. Each beer will have an info option that will display relevant info about the beer that is currently being shown.
    7. By clicking "where can i meet you" you will see locations on google maps of where you can get the beer.
